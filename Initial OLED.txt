Initial commands for OLED MT-6432TSWEG02 (only one mode: Page Addressing Mode)

808d 8014 [Activate internal power supply]

80B0      [Set Page Start] 		

80a1      [Set Segment Re-map] 		

80c8      [Set COM Output Scan Direction]

80d3 8060 [Set Display Offset]

8000      [Set Lower Column Start Address]

8012      [Set Higher Column Start Address]

8081 80ee [Set Contrast Control]

80af      [Set Display ON]
